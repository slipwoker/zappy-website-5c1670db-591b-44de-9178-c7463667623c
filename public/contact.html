Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about Metal Mania? We've got answers. If you need more info, our team typically responds within 24 hours—just include details about your event, venue, or booking inquiry.

What types of events does Metal Mania cover?

We specialize in heavy metal and hard rock concerts, festivals, album launches, and underground shows. From intimate club gigs to arena tours, we bring the energy and expertise to make your event unforgettable.

How far in advance should I book?

For major events and festivals, we recommend booking at least 3-6 months ahead. Smaller club shows can often be arranged with 4-6 weeks' notice, but earlier is always better to secure your preferred date.

Do you provide equipment and sound engineering?

Absolutely. We offer full production packages including PA systems, stage lighting, backline equipment, and experienced sound engineers who know how to make metal sound massive. Custom packages available based on venue size.

Can you help with artist booking and lineup curation?

Yes! We have connections with local and touring metal acts across multiple subgenres. Whether you need headliners, openers, or a full festival lineup, we'll curate a bill that matches your vision and budget.

What's your cancellation and refund policy?

Cancellations made 60+ days before the event receive a full refund minus a 10% admin fee. Within 30-60 days, 50% refund. Less than 30 days, deposits are non-refundable. Force majeure exceptions apply—contact us for details.

Do you offer payment plans for large events?

Yes. For events over $5,000, we offer flexible payment schedules: 30% deposit to secure the date, 40% at 60 days out, and final 30% two weeks before the event. Custom terms available for multi-day festivals.
